Biryani Recipe

Ingredients Requird

  • Meat
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic Paste
  • Ginger Paste
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Biryani Masala

Steps Of Cooking

  1. Fry onions in hot oil until golden.Add tomatoes and fry until oil seprates
  2. Add meat,garlic paste,yogurt and biryani masala.Stir fry for 10 minutes.Add water 2 cups.Cover and cook on low heat until meat is tender.Then increase heat and stir fry until oil seprates from gravy.

  3. Separately:

    In 15 cups/3 liters of boiling water,stir in 3 tablespoons of salt and soaked rice.Boil rice until 3/4 cooked.remove and drain throughly.
  4. Spred half rice in pot and pour meat curry.Top with remaining rice.Cover pot and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked (5-10 minutes).Mix before serving.

  5. Tips

    For meat,use breast,ribs & shoulder cuts.
